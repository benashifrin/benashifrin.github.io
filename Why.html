Why

In your 20s, finding your purpose is at the heart of most of our problems. After travelling Asia for 5 months in 2025 that was my main struggle. While I was in Turkey, I listened to this podcast and found my purpose. Essentially I will be using my gifts to build businesses and use those to serve the communities that have shaped me.
